## Short Description

AGI Summit 2026 is a two-day AI conference at the Palace of Fine Arts in San Francisco, bringing together builders, researchers, founders, investors, frontier labs, major technology companies, academic leaders, and startup teams. The event positions itself as one of Silicon Valley's largest and most influential AGI and GenAI gatherings, with 15,000+ expected attendees, 200+ expected speakers, 200+ exhibitors, 500+ VCs, and attendees from 30+ countries.

The central positioning is direct access to the people defining the next era of AI: researchers from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google DeepMind, Stanford, Berkeley, and MIT; operators from Microsoft, Meta, Tesla, NVIDIA, Apple, AWS, Coinbase, and Stripe; investors from firms such as a16z, Sequoia, Benchmark, Lightspeed, Founders Fund, BlackRock, Khosla Ventures, Premise VC, DCM Ventures, Menlo Ventures, and others; and founders building AI-native products, agent infrastructure, autonomous workflows, robotics, legal AI, healthcare AI, and enterprise AI systems.
